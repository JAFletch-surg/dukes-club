# The Dukes' Club — User Guide

A guide for members and visitors to The Dukes' Club website.

---

## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Click **Register** and fill in:
   - Full name, email, and password.
   - **Training stage** (FY1–ST8, Post-CCT, Consultant, SAS, Academic, or Other).
   - **Deanery / Region** (16 UK and Ireland regions).
   - **ACPGBI membership number** (optional — enter this to unlock Full Member features).
   - **Directory visibility** — choose whether to appear in the Member Directory.
2. Submit the form and check your email for a verification link.

### Auto-Approval
If you register with an NHS (.nhs.net, .nhs.uk), doctors.org.uk, or .ac.uk email address, your account is approved automatically as a **Trainee**. Other email domains require manual admin approval, which typically takes 1–2 working days.

### Logging In
Go to `/login` and enter your email and password. If you have forgotten your password, click **Forgot password?** to receive a reset link by email.

## Membership Tiers

| Tier | How to get it | What you can access |
|------|---------------|---------------------|
| **Guest** | No account needed | Public pages: Home, About, Contact, Events listing, News |
| **Trainee** | Register and get approved | Member portal, webinars, podcasts, directory, messaging, 7-day question bank trial |
| **Full Member** | Add a verified ACPGBI number on your profile | Everything above, plus: unlimited question bank, in-person course booking, certificates & CPD tracking |

## Public Pages

### Home
The homepage highlights featured events, latest news, membership benefits, testimonials, and sponsors.

### About
Learn about the club's mission, view executive committee profiles, download governance documents, and find regional representatives.

### Events
Browse upcoming events in **list** or **calendar** view. Filter by event type (Webinar, Workshop, Conference, etc.), subspecialty, or search by keyword. Each event card shows the date, location, price, and capacity.

### News
Read articles and announcements. Filter by category (Education, Careers, Research, Policy, etc.) or search by keyword.

### Contact
Send a message to the team using the contact form. Choose a subject category (Membership, Events, Sponsorship, etc.) and provide your details.

### Exams
Information about surgical exams and how to prepare.

## Member Portal

After logging in, you can access the member portal at `/members`.

### Dashboard
An overview of your activity: upcoming events, recent videos, exam prep stats, and quick links to key sections.

### Profile
Manage your account at `/members/profile`:

**Personal Info tab:**
- Update your name, hospital, region, training stage, subspecialty interests, and social links (Twitter/X, LinkedIn).

**ACPGBI Membership:**
- Enter your ACPGBI membership number to unlock Full Member access.
- Status shows as "Full Member" (verified), "Pending Verification", or a prompt to submit your number.

**Privacy Settings tab:**
- Toggle directory visibility on or off.
- When visible, control what others can see: email, hospital, region, training stage, subspecialties, social links, and whether you accept direct messages.

**Account tab:**
- Change your password.
- Delete your account (with confirmation).

## Events

### Browsing Events
Visit `/events` to see all upcoming events. Use the search bar, event type dropdown, and subspecialty filter to find what you need. Toggle between list and calendar views.

### Event Details
Click an event to see full details: description, timetable (single or multi-day), faculty and speakers, location with map link, and pricing (standard vs. member rate).

### Registering for Events

**Online events (Webinars, Online Lectures):**
1. Click **Register for Webinar**.
2. Once approved, a join button and meeting details (Zoom link, Meeting ID, Passcode) appear on the event page.

**Application-based events (Workshops, In Person Courses):**
1. Fill in the application form (motivation statement, answers to custom questions).
2. Submit your application before the deadline.
3. Check your status: Pending → Approved / Rejected / Waitlisted.
4. You will receive an email when your status changes.
5. If approved, confirm your attendance.

**In-person courses for Full Members:**
Some in-person courses require a verified ACPGBI membership number. Add your number in your profile to gain access.

### Cancelling
You can cancel a registration from the event page at any time.

## Videos

Access the video library at `/members/videos`. Browse operative videos, lectures, webinar recordings, and educational content.

- **Search** by title or speaker name.
- **Filter** by category, subspecialty, or difficulty.
- **Watch** directly on the page (Vimeo embed).
- **Track progress** — the system records which videos you have watched.

Videos are members-only content.

## Podcasts

Listen to podcast episodes at `/members/podcasts`.

- Browse episodes by title, guest name, or tag.
- Filter by subspecialty or predefined tags (Cancer, IBD, Robotic, etc.).
- Click **Listen** to expand the Spotify player and play the episode directly.
- View guest names, episode duration, and descriptions.

## Webinars

View upcoming and past webinars at `/members/webinars`.

**Upcoming webinars:**
- See the date, speakers, stream type (Zoom / Vimeo / Hybrid), and remaining spots.
- Register with one click.
- Once approved, the join button and meeting details appear with a countdown timer.
- Cancel your registration if needed.

**Past webinars:**
- Browse recordings of previous webinars.
- If a recording is available, click to watch it in the video library.

## FRCS Exam Preparation

### Exam Hub
Visit `/members/frcs` to see your exam prep dashboard:

- **Overall score** — Animated percentage showing your average.
- **Questions attempted** — Total count.
- **Day streak** — Current consecutive-day practice streak.
- **Best exam score** — Highest score on a simulated exam.
- **Percentile ranking** — See where you stand compared to other members via a score distribution chart.
- **Topic breakdown** — Performance by category (Colorectal, General Surgery, Foundations) with correct/attempted counts.

### Question Bank
Practice at `/members/questions`:

- **1,000+ MCQs** covering Colorectal, General Surgery, and Foundations topics.
- **Two practice modes:**
  - **Untimed practice** — Answer questions with immediate feedback and explanations.
  - **Exam simulation** — Timed test; feedback shown only at the end.
- **Filter** by topic, difficulty (Easy / Medium / Hard), and subspecialty.
- **Bookmark** difficult questions for later review.
- **Track performance** over time and compare with peers.
- **Flag questions** if you spot an issue (incorrect answer, outdated info, unclear wording, wrong topic, duplicate).

**Access:**
- Trainees get a **7-day free trial**.
- Full Members (with verified ACPGBI number) get **unlimited access**.

### Resources
Visit `/members/frcs/resources` for curated study materials, practice resources, textbooks, and external links.

## Certificates

Download your certificates at `/members/certificates`.

- Certificates are issued after you attend an event **and** complete the feedback form.
- Each certificate shows: event title, certificate type, date issued, CPD points (if applicable), and a verification code.
- Click **Download** to save the certificate as a PDF.
- If you have pending feedback to complete, a banner links you to the feedback form.

---

## Fellowships

Browse fellowship opportunities at `/members/fellowships`.

Each listing includes:
- Programme title and description.
- Duration (6 or 12 months).
- Subspecialties covered.
- Accreditation bodies.
- Supervisor information.
- Salary range and on-call pattern.
- Locations (hospitals and institutions).
- Application deadline and eligibility criteria.

---

## Member Directory

Find other members at `/members/directory`.

- Search by name, region, hospital, or subspecialty.
- Filter by region or training stage.
- View member cards showing name, training stage, region, hospital, subspecialty interests, and social links.
- Click **Message** to send a direct message (if the member allows it).
- Your own card is marked with a "You" label.

Only members who have enabled directory visibility in their profile settings appear here.

---

## Messages

Send and receive direct messages at `/members/messages`.

- Start a conversation from the Member Directory by clicking **Message** on a member's card.
- View your conversation history.
- Messages include timestamps and read receipts.
- You can only message members who have enabled messaging in their privacy settings.
